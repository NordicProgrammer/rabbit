Rabbits!

Why Rabbits are good pets.

  • They are lovable.
  • They are very smart.
  • They can be litter trained.

Basic Info

Rabbits are small animals that come from the Leporidae family. Rabbits are very smart creatures. They have their own very distinct personalities and can understand simple commands. Because they are prey animals rabbits generally do like to be picked up. They eat a deit of pellets, hay, and greens.

Myth-Busters

  1. Rabbits only eat carrots.
    Rabbits can not just eat carrots like they do in cartoons. The sugar content can make them sick. They love them though!
  2. Rabbits are smelly and dirty creatures.
    Rabbits are very clean creatures. They smell amazing! They do not like to be dirty.
  3. Rabbits are low matinence pets.
    Rabbits are not low matinence pets. They have to be fed everyday, their enclosure needs to be cleaned twice a week, and you have to let them out to run around everynight.
  4. Rabbits like to be cuddled.
    Rabbits do not like to be cuddled. If they don't feel secure when you pick them up they will kick hard. So hard that they might break their own spine.

How to Start

To start go to your local rabbit rescue. Ask them about rabbit bonding. They will show your rabbit to a few other rabbits. They are doing this to see which rabbit gets along best with your rabbit.

Bonding

When you have found a rabbit they will send you home with it so you can bond them at home. You bond them by putting them into a small pen and squish them together and stroke them.

Food

They have to be fed greens and pellets everyday. You also have to given them fresh hay and water every day.

Exercise

Bunnies need to be let out to run around the house so they can get exercise. They enjoy it alot!

Bunny Proofing

Before you let your rabbits out to run around the house you have to bunny proof the rooms that your rabbits will go in. You need to make sure there are no electrical wires that the rabbits could chew on. If they did manage to chew on it would give them a electric shock. Another thing you have to do to bunny proof is to block off spaces where they might get stuck. You also need to block off all wooden furniture because the rabbit will chew on it.
